Pizza Recipe

Description

This classic Pizza recipe is made with layers of rich meat sauce, creamy ricotta, and melted mozzarella cheese. Perfect for family dinners and special occasions!

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 (28 oz) can crushed tomatoes
  • 2 (6 oz) cans tomato paste
  • 2 (6.5 oz) cans canned tomato sauce

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a large skillet, cook ground beef, onion, and garlic over medium heat until well browned. Drain excess fat.
  3. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, and tomato sauce. Simmer for 30 minutes, stirring occasionally.

  5. In a 9x13 inch baking dish, spread a thin layer of meat sauce. Layer with noodles, ricotta mixture, mozzarella cheese, and meat sauce. Repeat layers.

  7. Let stand for 15 minutes before serving. Enjoy!
